About This Website:

This website is specially created for those who want to learn HTML linking and navigation. Here, you
will understand how to connect different pages, jump to specific sections within a page, and open
external websites in a new tab.

What You'll Find Here:

On this website, you'll discover five different pages, all linked together:

  1. Home Page | Your starting point to explore everything on this website.
  2. About Page | Learn more about why this website was created.
  3. Services Page | Find out what you can learn from this website.
  4. Contact Page | Reach out to me with feedback or questions.
  5. Gallery Page | Explore examples of HTML links and designs.

Why This Website?

If you're looking to understand HTML links and navigation, this website is a great place to start. You'll
get hands-on experience with relative links, absolute links, and internal page navigation.
